New Trends on Calculus of Variations and PDE's


Firenze-Montecatini
June 12-15, 2017

with a special tribute to Paolo Marcellini




The workshop aims to celebrate Paolo Marcellini in the occasion of his 70th birthday for the impact he had on the study and developments in Calculus of Variations and Partial Differential Equations. The participation of international experts in these topics is planned.

PRACTICAL INFORMATIONS

The first day session, on Monday June 12th 2017, is scheduled in Florence at the Aula Magna of the "Rettorato" of the University of Firenze (map). Then the conference will take place at Hotel Belvedere in Montecatini Terme (PT). A bus-trip will take the participants from the "Aula Magna" of the University of Firenze to the location in Montecatini (about 40 Km from Firenze) at the end of the lectures on Monday afternoon June 12th 2017. Remember to bring your baggage with you!

The meeting will take place from the afternoon of Monday June 12th to noon of Thursday June 15th, 3 nights, in Montecatini Terme (PT) at Hotel Belvedere: phone-number +39 0572-70251 email: belve@bk1.it Participants can reserve directly the Hotel at the phone-number +39 0572-911864 or email: info@bk1.it (at the attention to Mr. Daniele Morosi and/or to Mrs. Silvia Bechini).
