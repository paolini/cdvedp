This conference represents the first collaboration between the Department of Mathematics U. Dini, hence of the University of Firenze, with scientists and mathematicians of Argentina Republic, a country which is, according to geography, far from Italy, but in reality very close for its culture and habits.

The present conference is part of a project, partially funded by the Ministero degli Affari Esteri of Italian government, that aims to establish a technological and scientific collaboration between Italian and Argentine mathematician about various topics on pure and applied mathematics.
